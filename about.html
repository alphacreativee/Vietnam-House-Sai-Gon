Our Story

Vietnam House was conceived as a contemporary expression of Vietnamese culinary heritage - where tradition is respected, not replicated. Drawing from regional flavours across Vietnam, the restaurant presents familiar dishes through refined technique, balance, and restraint. Each experience reflects a dialogue between culture and craft, shaped by a shared philosophy of authenticity, precision, and thoughtful reinterpretation.

Chef Luke Nguyễn

An internationally acclaimed chef, author, and cultural ambassador, Luke Nguyen has played a defining role in bringing Vietnamese cuisine to the global stage. His culinary philosophy bridges heritage and modern refinement, shaped by decades of exploration across Vietnam and beyond. At Vietnam House, Chef Luke contributes a deeply personal interpretation of Vietnamese cuisine, where craftsmanship, storytelling, and cultural respect converge.

  • Inducted into the Food Hall of Fame, with culinary programs broadcast in over 150 countries

  • Author of seven award-winning cookbooks on Vietnamese and Asian gastronomy

  • Visionary chef behind Red Lantern and Fat Noodle, critically acclaimed dining destinations

  • Food Ambassador for Etihad Airways and host of MasterChef Vietnam

Vietnamese Heritage on the Plate
At Vietnam House Restaurant, Vietnamese heritage is expressed not through nostalgia, but through refinement. Each dish carries the essence of regional flavours, carefully reimagined with modern technique and precision. Rooted in tradition yet elevated in form, every plate becomes a quiet tribute to Vietnam’s culinary identity - timeless, elegant, and deeply authentic.
